About

The Best Irish Pub

Dublin, a city of life, hospitality and fun, has a pub culture that is famous throughout the world. Dublin is Ireland’s capital and Europe’s most treasured city. It is steeped in tradition and history.

Now, San Diego has its very own piece of Dublin, snug in the corner of the Gaslamp Quarter’s Fourth Avenue. The Dubliner was manufactured by O’Sullivan Interiors and shipped thousands of miles across the Atlantic Ocean. It is an authentic Irish pub exuding the warm hospitality and fun that is typically Irish.
